Queen's Engineering Competition (QEC) is an intensive two-day engineering challenge open to all years, bringing together a diverse range of disciplines and skillsets. QEC showcases the innovation and creativity of Queenâ€™s Engineering students, throughout a day of good-natured competition.

The 2018 competition will focus on consulting and engineering design.. The winning teams of each category will receive the conference grand prize as well as a fully payed trip to compete in the Ontario Engineering Competition (OEC). Qualifying teams will compete for cash prizes and a chance to attend the Canadian Engineering Competition (CEC). Speakers and sponsors have been invited to share industry experience and to view the talents in Queen's Engineering.

Senior Design

Participants work in teams to address technological engineering problems using Robotic kits. Each Team is evaluated on features including novelty, practicality, & usability.

Junior Design

Participants work in teams to develop a solution to a creative design problem using affordable, everyday materials. Teams are evaluated on criteria including effectiveness, budget, and design.

Consulting Case

Participants work in teams to develop a solution to a real life engineering-consulting problem that requires consideration of the environmental, economical, and technical aspects. The consulting case will be judged by Isaac Consulting.
