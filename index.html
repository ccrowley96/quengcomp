Queen's Engineering Competition

Quis Dolor Cui Dolium


QEC is an annual conference that allows engineering students from different disciplines to compete in a variety of competitions involving many aspects of engineering.

The 2017 competition will focus on consulting, engineering design, and coding. This year the event is growing. Speakers and sponsors have been invited to share industry experience and to view the talents in Queen's Engineering.
